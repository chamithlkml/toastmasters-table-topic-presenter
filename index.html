Qualities of the Independence


Sovereignty

The right to govern a nation without interference from other nations.

Self-Sufficiency

The ability to survive on our own without much help from other nations.

Integrity of the Person

The right to be exclusive controller of one's own body, mind and life.

The freedom to communicate opinions and ideas without fear of being punished.
